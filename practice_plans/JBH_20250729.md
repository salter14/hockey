- Knee downs on each stride
- c-cuts right
- c-cuts left
- backwards c-cuts
- outside edges (big cross overs) 
- backwards to forwards to backwards transitions skating down the ice
- stops
- tight turns
- red light / green light

- forward/backward transitions changing directions
